Design notes

  • A — Slider: More tactile, feels interactive, good for on-site QR demos. Harder to use precisely on mobile.
  • B — Chips: Faster tap, works better on mobile, cleaner. Less "interactive toy" feeling. Recommended for real use.
  • Both show progress dots (step 2 of 4) — sets expectation that this is quick
  • Both ask exactly 2 questions — bill range + ownership
  • Ownership question uses the actual address for personalization
